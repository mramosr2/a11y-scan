Privacy Policy for Web Accessibility Auditor - Alt Text & WCAG GPT

Effective date: September 20, 2025

Who we are

This service powers a custom GPT Action that audits public webpages for accessibility issues and drafts high-quality alt text. The project is operated by Michael Ramos with a focus on practical, developer-ready WCAG guidance.

Our mission is simple: accelerate accessible design by returning actionable findings you can drop into code or content with minimal friction.

What this Action does

When you submit a URL, the Action fetches the page in a headless browser and runs axe-core checks. It then returns a structured JSON report including alt-text suggestions, HTML and Markdown snippets, WCAG references, and optional contrast checks intended to speed remediation and documentation.

The scanner targets public pages only. It does not authenticate, access gated content, or bypass access controls.

Information we process

  • Submitted URL. Needed to perform the scan and generate findings.
  • Page content. Temporarily processed in memory to compute results such as WCAG issues, alt-text drafts, and snippets.
  • Technical logs. Minimal diagnostics (timestamps, duration, status codes, errors) to keep the service reliable.
No accounts or cookies are used by this API. We do not request names, addresses, or payment details.

Data storage & retention

Scan results are generated on demand and returned in the response. The service does not persist page content or full reports after responding. Minimal server logs may be retained for up to 30 days for reliability, performance tuning, and abuse prevention, and longer only if required for security investigations.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited operational data may be shared with infrastructure providers solely to run and protect the service. These providers are contractually restricted from using data for unrelated purposes.

Security

All transport uses HTTPS. The headless browser runs in an isolated environment. The system avoids long-term storage of page content, limiting exposure. Dependencies are updated regularly and monitored for high-severity vulnerabilities.

No internet service can guarantee absolute security. We minimize retained data and practice least privilege to reduce risk.

Your choices

Only scan URLs you are authorized to test and that do not contain private or sensitive content. If you believe something was processed in error, contact us and we will investigate and remove associated logs where applicable.
